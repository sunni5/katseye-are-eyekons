Who are the members?

-Manon

Age: 23yo

Nationality: Half Swiss-Half Ghanian

Before Fame: Model for Companies such as Dermalogica, and was a popular Social Media personality (this helped her get scouted for Dream Academy!)

Fun Facts: Her favorite show is Stranger Things! (perfect since season 5 is coming out...)

-Sophia

Age: 22yo

Nationality: Filipino

Before Fame: She was on Philippine Talk Shows, as her parents were quite well-known and could give her these opportunities

Fun Facts: She didn't tell her parents she applied to Dream Academy until she was preparing to leave!

-Daniela

Age: 21yo

Nationality: Cuban-American

Before Fame: She was on America's Got Talent at only 9 years old, showing off her balldancing skills! She performed many times as a professional dancer prior to Dream Academy.

Fun Facts: Her favorite song is "Guess" by Charlie XCX

-Lara

Age: 20yo

Nationality: Indian-American

Before Fame: She starred in commericals, including Michelle Obama's Global Girl Alliance Ad

Fun Facts: Her sister, Rhea Raj, is also an aspiring performer and is a solo artist of her own!

-Megan

Age: 19yo

Nationality: Chinese-Swedish and American Born

Before Fame: She was a child actress in films such as "Sydney to the Max" "Bosch: Legacy" and played a part in Jurassic World, though the credits are unspecified.

Fun Facts: In her privated TikTok, you can find all the old vlogs she used to make prior to Dream Academy, which are all very entertaining!

-Yoonchae

Age: 17yo

Nationality: South Korean

Before Fame: She was a student in South Korea, dreaming to become a KPop Idol and had broken English (it has gotten a lot better since she debuted!)

Fun Facts: She debuted at only 16 years old, making her the youngest person to be nominated for a Grammy Nominee when Katseye got nominated earlier this year.
